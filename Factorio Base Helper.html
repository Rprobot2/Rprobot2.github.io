Factorio Base Helper

Factorio is a fantastic game that I have devoted many hours to. It's got a depth that few games can match, a fantastic modding community, and the best devs I've ever seen. Factorio, in essence, is a sandbox factory game, where you automate the production of items, in order to create items (that then help you automate the production of items). In-game, there are a multitude of things to consider when building your factory. One of the most common ones is how many factories against how many belts; what ratio works well. For example, 7 factories may require 1.1 belts and 6 factories 0.9: in this case, you'd have to place down another belt entirely, which consumes only 10% of its capacity, just to transport the 7th factory's output.

In Factorio, efficiency is key. As such, I figured that for my year 12 major project I could develop a decision support system (DSS) to help players determine how many belts they need, and they can then choose what tier of belt they will use (they have varying speeds).

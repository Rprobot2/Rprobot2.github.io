IPTAnalysis

This was done as part of my IPT work - a minor project, where we worked as a team of three to create something in an area of intrigue. We decided that a stock market UI would be pretty nice, and also enable us to learn some further R. Matt and I programmed the project, with him mainly working on the backend and I the frontend. Max worked on most of the documentation and keeping track on the team's progress. In the end, it serves two primary purposes. The first, of course, being a graph of stock market data, with a multitude of configurables. The second feature, which I devoted much of my time to, scans for news articles related to your search, provides you with their date of publish, and hyperlinks them. Hence this website could be very useful for a prospectful investor as it provides a basic (and simple; streamlined) interface to the stock market.

  • IPTAnalysis, hosted online

We used numerous libraries in order to webscrape both the stock market data and the news articles. I really enjoyed R because of it's specific focus on data science. I found it a really 'clean' (if such a word can be used) language, especially for this web app. Also, Shiny was really, really amazing. It was an incredibly useful library, and makes the app look fantastic.
